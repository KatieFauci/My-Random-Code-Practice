Classic Lasagna

A hearty and flavorful classic lasagna made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and marinara sauce.

Servings: 8

Prep Time: 30

Cooking Time: 45

Ingredients:

  • 12 sheets Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 pound Ground Beef
  • 4 cups Marinara Sauce
  • 15 ounces Ricotta Cheese
  • 2 cups, shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 cup, grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 cloves, minced Garlic
  • 2 tablespoons Olive Oil
  • to taste Salt
  • to taste Black Pepper
  • 1 teaspoon Italian Seasoning

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add ground beef, season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning; cook until browned.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce and let simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and a pinch of salt.
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Layer 4 lasagna noodles over the sauce, then spread half of the ricotta mixture on top.
  8. Add a layer of mozzarella cheese and more meat sauce.
  9. Repeat the layers (noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, meat sauce) two more times.
  10. Finish with a layer of noodles, remaining meat sauce, and top with mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  11. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  13. Let stand for 10 minutes before slicing.
