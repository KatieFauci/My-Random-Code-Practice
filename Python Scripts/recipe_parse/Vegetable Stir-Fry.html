Vegetable Stir-Fry

A colorful and nutritious vegetable stir-fry, perfect for a quick and healthy meal.

Servings: 4

Prep Time: 15

Cooking Time: 10

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups, chopped Broccoli
  • 1 large, sliced Bell Pepper
  • 1 large, sliced Carrot
  • 1 cup Snow Peas
  • 1 medium, sliced Onion
  • 3 cloves, minced Garlic
  • 1 tablespoon, grated Ginger
  • 3 tablespoons Soy Sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Sesame Oil
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • for garnish (optional) Sesame Seeds

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger; saut√© for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add sliced onion and cook for 2 minutes until softened.
  4. Add broccoli, bell pepper, carrot, and snow peas; stir-fry for about 5-7 minutes, or until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Pour in soy sauce and sesame oil; toss to coat the vegetables evenly.
  6. Cook for another minute to heat through.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with sesame seeds if desired.
