Chocolate Chip Cookies

Classic chocolate chip cookies with a crispy edge and chewy center.

Servings: 24

Prep Time: 15

Cooking Time: 12

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Butter
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 cups Chocolate Chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together butter and sugars until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  4. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt; stir into butter mixture until blended.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden.
